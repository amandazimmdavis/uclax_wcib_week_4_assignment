Kayaking. Perfect for beginners to get out on the water.

We offer both single and double kayaks, including some with glass bottoms so that you can see the wildlife beneath you. Kayaks are lighter and eaiser to handle, perfect for those just starting out or for someone who's been kayaking for years.

SUP. More of a challenge but a great workout and stunning views.

SUP came on the scene and took bodies of water by storm. With a small learning curve, once you get the hang of it, you'll never look back. It's a great way to see the water and also a great full-body workout.

Canoeing. Perfect for those who want a group activity.

Canoes are great for those who want to be on the water with more people. They're perfect for being on the lake or heading to your favorite fishing spot.
